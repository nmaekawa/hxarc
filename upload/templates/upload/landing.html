Welcome to hxarc v{{ hxarc_version }}

This is the web version of Colin Fredericks' hx_util (course export utilities).

Upload your edX export (<edx-random-id>.tar.gz) to get:

  • a list of all the video files in your course and where in the course they appear in .tsv format (Course_Video_Sheet.tsv)
  • a folder full of .srt transcript files
  • a list of all URLs to external sites and documents used in the course
  • more good stuff as Colin adds more pixie dust

Upload returns a page with a download link and the file you'll get back will be called hxarc_<bunchacharacters>.tar.gz and opening that will give you the above items.

If you need to open the .tsv, using Google Sheets will reduce Unicode issues that Excell can suffer.
